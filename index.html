About Me

Welcome to my mapping portfolio! I specialize in creating interactive and visually stunning maps for various applications. With a passion for geography and data visualization, I bring maps to life.

Projects

Cassava Planting Plan Map

This project was carried out by selecting areas within former, unproductive Industrial Plantation Forests (HTI). The selection focused on zones with gentle slopes, suitable for cassava cultivation based on agronomic requirements. Once suitable areas were identified, spatial blocking was conducted, including the layout of planned planting rows optimized for cassava planting mechanization.

Imagery Block Score Card for Palm Oil Areas

This project involves the creation of drone aerial orthophotos for my client. Our offerings include drone aerial orthophotography, land cover analysis, hectare statements of location, palm counting, infrastructure mapping, and layout design for print-out purposes.

SKT Map Plan

The objective of this project was to produce a detailed landcover classification and spatial block design for a proposed plasma plantation area. The process began by utilizing the available land bank to design plantation blocks, carefully aligning block naming conventions with those of adjacent core plantation blocks to maintain operational coherence. The initial block design was further refined through spatial subdivision to allocate areas for road infrastructure and planting zones. Each planting zone was then subdivided into 2-hectare units to streamline the application process for individual land ownership certificates (SHM) for future plasma farmers.

Leaflet Covid-19 Ketapang Map

This project is a web-based leaflet choropleth map visualization that displays the distribution of COVID-19 cases across villages and districts in Ketapang Regency, West Kalimantan. The map includes information about village and district data, employee data, and assists management in visually estimating the probability of COVID-19 spread.

Topography Map

This project involves the development of a detailed topographic map derived from Digital Elevation Model (DEM) data for the Birang area under the management of PT TRH in East Kalimantan. The DEM was processed to generate slope information, expressed in percentage (%), to better understand the terrain characteristics of the region. The final map layout includes essential geographic features such as administrative boundaries, river networks, and road infrastructure, providing a comprehensive spatial overview. This map is designed to support field surveys and facilitate quick interpretation of the areaâ€™s topography, enhancing operational planning and decision-making for the company.

DEM Analysis

Using drone-captured orthophotos, a Digital Surface Model (DSM) will be generated. By overlaying the orthophoto, DSM, and an existing Digital Elevation Model (DEM), the analysis will identify the natural flow paths and lowest elevation points. This integrated elevation analysis will support the planning and alignment of canals, ensuring they follow optimal drainage routes based on the natural topography.
